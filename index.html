About Mac DeMarco

Biography

Mac DeMarco (born Vernor Winfield McBriare Smith IV on April 30, 1990) is a Canadian singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and producer. Known for his eclectic and laid-back musical style, he has released several critically acclaimed albums and developed a dedicated international following.

Musical Style

DeMarco's music has been described as "blue wave", "slacker rock", and "jangle pop". His sound incorporates elements of:

  • Lo-fi recording techniques
  • Jazz-influenced chord progressions
  • Dreamy, reverb-drenched guitars
  • Off-kilter lyrical themes
  • DIY production aesthetic

Instruments & Equipment

  1. Guitars: Teisco Del Rey, Fender Stratocaster
  2. Amps: Polytone Mini-Brute, Fender Twin Reverb
  3. Keyboards: Yamaha DX7, Roland Juno-60
  4. Recording: Tascam 4-track cassette recorder
  5. Effects: Boss VB-2 Vibrato, Boss CE-2 Chorus
